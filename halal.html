HALAL EATING PLACES

ALI OCAKBASI RESTAURANT

Location: Herengracht 558/A, 1017 CG Amsterdam

You can find numerous halal options there. Ali Ocakbaşı offers its guests unique flavors with the most natural aromas grilled on the fireside or in wood fire ovens. It aims to offer a new generation fireside and kebab pleasure in Amsterdam.

MERAM RESTAURANT

Location: Haringpakkerssteeg 8, 1012 LR Amsterdam

It has delicious steaks such as rib-eye, spare ribs and various other meat dishes and yes they are halal!! There are also many alternatives of Turkish cuisine available for vegetarians.
