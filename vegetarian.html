EATING PLACE FOR VEGETARIANS

CAFE DE CEUVEL,

Location: Korte Papaverweg 4, 1032 KB Amsterdam

This exceptional café was opened in 2014 with a focus on providing healthy food not only for people, but for the planet, too. From the composting toilets to the recycling facilities, there’s simply no waste (or wasted appetite) at De Ceuvel. You can have vegetarian and vegan flat breads, soups, snacks, pastas and salads.
